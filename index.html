Alex Meinhof


I am a graduate student in the Department of Philosophy at the University of Wisconsin, Madison.

I am interested in the intersection of philosophy of language, philosophy of science, and metaphysics.

I make music: solo (under my own name), and as one half of a duo (under the name ’20.8(3) Microseconds’).
